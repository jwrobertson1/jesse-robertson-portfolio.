About

I spent 10 years managing dispatch and fleet operations at Solar Transport — 9 terminals, 7 states, up to 150 drivers. I reduced service failures from 800+ per year to under 100 and built a safety culture that earned 19 consecutive fleet safety awards.

Along the way I started solving operational problems with AI before most people knew what a prompt was. I built HR workflows, compliance systems, and reporting tools that replaced entire headcount. Then I decided to build products out of it.

Under AI Business Systems, I've shipped HR Shield, DOT Shield, and RepairFlow — three AI-powered tools addressing real SMB pain points, all built and deployed solo. The DOT Shield concept includes a Phase 2 ELD integration that gives dispatchers real-time HOS answers in plain English — a product I believe has serious potential in the fleet management space.

I hold a Full Stack certificate from UT Austin and build with Claude API, JavaScript, Node.js, Vercel, and Google Apps Script.
